  • 3D Mapping: Point Cloud- Scan to BIM for As-Built

    Leveraging laser scanning and advanced modeling techniques, we empower architects, engineers, and project managers with precise as-built data, enhancing project planning, reducing errors, and streamlining renovation, construction, and facility management processes. 

    Scan to BIM for Reality Capture and As-Built

    A point cloud is a set of data points in space. Point clouds are produced by 3D laser scanners, which measure a large number of points on the external surfaces of objects around them. Point Clouds are a collection of points that represent a 3D shape or feature.
    We can provide this, Scan to BIM support for renovation projects, heritage documentation and as well as for Greenfield projects as well, where we capture as-built for quality check for work put in place. We provide both scanning and modelling of facilities.

    LASER SCANNING SURVEY:

    Laser scanning is a fast and precise survey technology for capturing the as-built status of assets and creating 3D data. It combines accurate dimension control with 360° scanning, producing point clouds with ±3mm accuracy. These models support 2D validation, clash detection, tie-in point identification, and facility management.

    Software's for 3D scanning:

    Faro scene, Cyclone modeller, Autucad plant 3D, PDMS, Cadmatic, Autocad Blander, Revit, SOLIDWORK Siemens UX
