Building & Industrial Audits

Our audits are designed to ensure compliance, enhance safety, and optimize operational efficiency across diverse sectors. Tailored to industry standards, we cover every critical aspect of building and industrial safety and functionality.

BIM: Building Information Modeling

Our BIM expertise spans architecture, structural design, MEP systems, and project planning, ensuring seamless integration and enhanced efficiency. Whether it’s CAD-to-BIM migration or delivering sustainable solutions, we help achieve safer project outcomes.

Point Cloud: 3D Mapping

Leveraging laser scanning and advanced modeling techniques, we empower architects, engineers, and project managers with precise as-built data, enhancing project planning, reducing errors, and streamlining renovation, construction, and facility management processes. 

GIS Services

We provide accurate spatial data for construction, planning, and resource management. With advanced LiDAR mapping, GIS analysis, precision surveying, & drone surveys, we deliver actionable insights, enabling safer, more efficient project execution and optimized land development.

Rebar & Precast Detailing

We offer wide range of rebar sizes, diameters, and strength levels to suit different construction needs. Our expertise in breaking down rebar elements into precise bending shapes, lengths, steel grades, and diameters ensures efficient material use and minimizes construction errors.

Cost Management

By providing expert advice during the planning stage and throughout the project, we help you achieve maximum value for money. Our collaborative approach guarantees that your project stays within budget without compromising quality, ensuring timely completion and financial efficiency.

MEPF Design, Detailing and Engineering

Our MEPF Design and Engineering service optimizes building systems for efficiency and sustainability. Using advanced tools, we align designs with client priorities like cost and performance, reduce energy use, integrate renewables, and minimize CO2 emissions, delivering eco-friendly, cost-effective solutions.

Design Services: Architectural & Structural

We maximize property potential and value through a holistic approach: analyzing client needs and market trends, creating functional and ambiance-driven designs tailored to workflows, and ensuring seamless execution with cost management, land liaisoning, approvals, and efficient documentation for timely, budget-friendly delivery.

MEPF Services

Our MEPF services enhance building functionality, efficiency, and safety with optimized systems for plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and sustainable energy. Expert consultants adapt to demand cycles, utilizing off-peak periods for research and training to deliver top-quality results year-round.

Design & Execution Management

We ensure a seamless transition from concept to completion in construction projects. By coordinating the design (including architectural and engineering details) and construction, we ensure every project is executed efficiently, meeting timelines, budgets, and quality standards.
